### About us
Career Science Lab (CSL) is an interdisciplinary research institute of BOSS Zhipin, the largest online recruitment platform in China, with a focus on developing responsible, effective and efficient theories and technologies for cutting-edge career-centered applications. CSL has a diverse research team with Al scientists, economists, psychologists and experienced engineers from world-renowned universities, and is building a global collaboration network of innovation with top universities and institutes. The major research areas of CSL include *responsible Al*, *knowledge-enhanced pretrained models*, *labor economics*, *talent and management computing*, and *the next-generation technologies of intelligent recruitment*.

**Mission:** To make better development of people through career science research

**Vision:** To become a world-leading interdisciplinary research institute that contributes to the development of human through an in-depth understanding or career

**Values:** To conduct cutting-edge responsible research with human-centered; to create values for partners through both academic research and industrial applications
